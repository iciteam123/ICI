Innovative Compliance, Inc. is a trusted engineering and program management consulting firm serving the energy and water industries, with extensive experience spanning the full oil & gas lifecycle—from upstream production to downstream distribution—as well as comprehensive knowledge of gas and electric utility operations. As a certified DBE, MBE, and WBE business, our mission is to serve our clients by delivering high-quality services with reliable results.

Our Services

Project Management

We lead projects with clear, consistent communication at every stage, ensuring projects remain on scope, schedule, and budget. 

Engineering & Design

Our engineers provide reliable and competent design solutions helping clients get it right the first time, minimizing costly rework and project delays.

Regulatory Compliance

We navigate complex regulatory frameworks with precision to ensure compliance with all federal, state, and local requirements. 

Integrity Management 

We implement data-driven integrity management programs that proactively identify threats, mitigate risks, and ensure pipeline safety and reliability.

Environmental Services

Our environmental team provides detailed reporting and proactive permitting support to minimize ecological impact while minimizing project delays.
